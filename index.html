How to Play

Bunny Game is a puzzle game. The board is filled with five icons: blue bunny, red bunny, mushroom, cabbage, and carrot. Click any group of two or more matching icons that are connected north, south, east, or west — not diagonal — to clear them. When you clear a group, tiles fall down to fill the gaps. If a column empties, the remaining columns shift left. Your goal is to clear the entire board. Your score is how many tiles remain — lower is better. Zero is a perfect clear. The game ends when no valid moves remain.
Bomb Mode uses the same board and the same icons, but one square contains a bomb. The bomb always starts near the top, so you have the full board to work with. Your goal is to get the bomb to the bottom row. To clear tiles you need a group of three or more — pairs don't count. Clear tiles strategically to make the bomb fall. The bomb falls with gravity just like regular tiles, and stays with its column when columns shift. You cannot click the bomb. You win when the bomb reaches the bottom row. You lose when no valid moves remain and the bomb hasn't made it.
86 Bunnies is a puzzle game. The board is filled with four icons: blue bunny, mushroom, cabbage, and carrot. Your goal is to eliminate all the blue bunnies. You cannot click blue bunnies directly — you have to clear other icons strategically so that columns collapse and bring blue bunnies together. Once two or more blue bunnies are adjacent, you can click them to clear them. The game ends when all blue bunnies are gone or no moves remain. Your score is how many blue bunnies are left.
