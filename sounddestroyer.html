Sound Destroyer gives you a grid of 100 monsters and a frequency weapon. Each shot: aim at a column, listen to a tone, then match it as precisely as you can with the dial. The closer your match, the more columns your laser destroys — a perfect score wipes the full width. Clear all 100 monsters before your shots run out and you advance. Run out of shots with monsters still standing and it's game over. As rounds increase, you get fewer shots and less time to hear the tone. Make sure your volume is on — your ears are your weapon.

⚠️ Turn your volume on — this game requires sound.
