Macigame

Macigame was a puzzle game for the Apple Macintosh, popular in the late 1990s and early 2000s. The board was a grid filled with five icons — a blue bunny, a red bunny, a mushroom, a cabbage, and a carrot. You clicked groups of two or more matching icons connected north, south, east, or west to clear them. Tiles fell down to fill gaps. Empty columns collapsed left. The goal was to clear the entire board. Your score was how many tiles remained.

Macigame no longer exists as a native Mac application. But the game lives on.

Bunny Game is a free browser-based reimagining of Macigame with the same icons, the same grid, the same rules, and a second mode where you guide a bomb to the bottom of the board. No download. No install. Plays in any modern browser.
