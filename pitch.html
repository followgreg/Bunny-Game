Pitch plays you a pure sine wave tone. Your job is to turn the dial until you match it exactly. As you turn, you'll hear your current frequency in real time — use your ears to zero in. You can replay the target tone as many times as you need before submitting. Three rounds, scored by how close you get. Perfect score is 300.

⚠️ Turn your volume on before playing — this game is entirely sound-based.
